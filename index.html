Game instruction

  • in your turn -roll the dice [at least one] and accumalte the result in current
  • you can roll again or click hold to save the points from current and end your turn
  • Note ! if you got 6.6 - you will lose all points from "currents" and the turn will go to your opponent
  • if you managed to reach exactly the target sorce - you win ! if you passed it -you loose
